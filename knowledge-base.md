## Cyprus Investment Benefits

### Financial
- **EU membership**: Cyprus is a full EU member state
- **Favourable taxation**: Attractive tax residency options
- **19% VAT refund**: Available on registered resort properties (like Elysia Blu)
- **5% VAT option**: Available on primary residence purchases (Beachside, Konia Green, Pafilia Plaza)
- **Fast-appreciating market**: One of the Mediterranean's strongest property markets

### Permanent Residency Programme
- Minimum investment: EUR 300,000 in property
- Covers: spouse, dependent children (up to age 25), parents
- Validity: permanent (even after children age out)
- Residency requirement: only 1 day every 2 years
- EU benefits: freedom to live throughout the EU, eligible for EU citizenship after 7 years
- Timeline: approval within 2 months
- No language, medical, or interview requirements
- Approval rate: 100%

### Lifestyle
- 340 days of sunshine per year
- One of Europe's safest communities
- Rich history and culture
- World-class dining, golf, wellness, and beaches
- English widely spoken
- Excellent healthcare and education
